Why Lou?

The story behind the balm

Lou Started With One Problem.

Female runners were still using products that never spoke to where they actually chafed. The labels listed "inner thigh" if you were lucky. Nobody mentioned the bra line, the nipples, the armpits, or between the legs.

Every brand was either built for men and repackaged, or built for the bathroom shelf — flowery, soft, and completely wrong for a long run in the rain.

Lou's Rub Relief is a performance product first. Simple formula. Six zones. One application. No fluff, no flowers, no pretending the uncomfortable zones don't exist.

Made in small batches in the UK. Every ingredient listed. Safety assessed before a single unit ships.
